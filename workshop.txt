description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=In this mod I try to make everything [b]as Vanilla as possible[/b].
description=- Dispenser have 250 unit of water like the vanilla dispenser.
description=- Water Jug can be filled with gasoline for a total of 250 units.
description=- Vanilla Dispenser automatically converted to custom when loaded in the world.
description=- Dispenser start with random amount of water.
description=- Time to take and place bottle depend on the amount of water in it.
description=- New disabler mod to revert to vanilla water dispenser.
description=
description=[h2]Disable/Revert to vanilla[/h2]
description=This feature will only work for game 41.72+.
description=- Enable `WaterDispenserDisabler` mod (keep it enabled) and disable `WaterDispenser` mod.
description=- When a custom dispenser is found it will revert back to vanilla.
description=- This mod must be activated alone and kept enabled to revert the custom dispenser back to vanilla.
description=- Added this disabler mod for people who would want to stop using water dispenser.
